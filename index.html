POLICY


Foreign lashes must be removed prior to service.
If requesting free patch test, must book at least 24 hours prior to service.
Aftercare instructions must be followed to provide extension longevity and lash health.
During the duration of service, eyes must remain closed. Gum chewing, texting, talking will cause taped under eye gel pads to move and expose bottom lashes.
Due to safety reasons, services are for clients only - all guests, including children, are not allowed in service room due to potential hazards that could greatly compromise service.
Deposits must be paid for when booking appointment.
Arrive 5-10 minutes early to find parking, find studio and use restroom.
Appointments will allow up to 20 minutes late of time. If late less than 20 minutes appointment end time will not increase.
Rescheduling & cancellations must be given 24 hour notice otherwise forfeit deposit.
No shows will require to pay for 100% of service prior to booking.
A complimentary touch-up is available 3 days after service (must send photos prior on touch-up).
There are no refunds to any services provided.
